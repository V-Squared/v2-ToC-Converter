ToC Converter v02

App to convert your UL HTML ToC (Table of Contents) to JSON and YAML.

Instructions:

  1. Paste your ToC in form of HTML unordered list into the text fieldd below.
  2. Press the CONVERT button.
  3. Click into text field of JSON or YAML, select all text, copy it to clipboard.
  4. Paste it into your publishing system. In our case paste the YAML contents into Article Front Matter.

If you are interested in the source code: GitHub > V-Squared > v2-ToC-Converter. Report Bugs or Feature Requests: GitHub > V-Squared > Issue
